Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Red & White Skill Education?

Red & White Skill Education is a leading training institute that provides industry-focused courses designed to help students build practical skills and advance their careers.

2. Who can enroll in the courses?

Students, graduates, job seekers, and working professionals can enroll in our courses based on their interests, career goals, and eligibility requirements.

3. Do I need prior experience to join?

No. Many of our beginner-level courses are designed for learners with no prior experience, while advanced courses may require basic knowledge.

4. Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes. We offer placement assistance, including resume building, interview preparation, and career guidance to help students become job-ready.

5. Will I receive a certificate after completing the course?

Yes. Students who successfully complete their course requirements receive a course completion certificate.
